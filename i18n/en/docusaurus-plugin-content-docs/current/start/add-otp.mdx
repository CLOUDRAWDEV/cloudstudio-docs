1. Click the `Add OTP` button under `My Page > Account`, and enter the password required for sign in.

<img width="100%" src={otp08} alt="otp img" />

<p>&nbsp;</p>

2. `Install the Google Authenticator App` using the link provided.

<img width="100%" src={otp01} alt="otp img" />

<p>&nbsp;</p>

3. Open Google Authenticator been installed then `scan QR code`.

<img width="100%" src={otp02} alt="otp img" />

<p>&nbsp;</p>

4. The secret key will be provided only once so write down and keep codes safe.

<img width="100%" src={otp03} alt="otp img" />

<p>&nbsp;</p>

5. Enter a `verification code` from the Google Authenticator app, then click the `Completed` button.

<img width="100%" src={otp04} alt="otp img" />

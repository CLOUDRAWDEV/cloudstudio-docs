CloudStudio provides AI reviews for the current infrastructure. AI reviews provide analysis of infrastructure structures,
resource interconnections, security vulnerabilities with improvement recommendations, estimated costs,
and cost optimization strategies to help users build a more efficient and secure cloud environment.

<p>&nbsp;</p>

## AI Review

1. To conduct an AI Review, draw the desired infrastructure and click the `AI Review` button.

<div className={styles.imgWrap}>
    <img src={review01} width="800px" alt="배포이미지" />
</div>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

2. The AI review will run automatically, and the results will be displayed.
   The results screen is organized into five categories: `Infrastructure Analysis`, `Security Review`, `Cost Optimization`, `Optimization and Improvement Suggestions`, and `Summary`.

<div className={styles.imgWrap}>
    <img src={review02} width="800px" alt="배포이미지" />
</div>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

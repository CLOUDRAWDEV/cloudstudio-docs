1. AI Builder feature has been added.

    - Instead of designing infrastructure by dragging and dropping resource icons, the AI Builder allows you to design infrastructure through simple text input.
    - AI Builder analyzes the given requirements, suggests optimized cloud resources, and automatically generates deployment diagrams.
    - The architecture generated by AI Builder can be further modified and refined by the user.
